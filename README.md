# Meetup

Meeting With Your Friends And Organising Meetings Simplified

# Feautures

-   Start Or Join A Meeting
-   Video Calling
-   Togle Audio Streams
-   Toggle Video Streams
-   Multi User
-   Screen Sharing
-   Screen Recording
-   Rooms
-   Chatting
-   See Your Previous Meetings
-   Schedule Your Meetings
-   Delete Your Previous Meetings From Record
